Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how MediCure collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, contact, gender, date of birth, etc.
  • Medical Data: Symptoms, health history, diet and exercise preferences.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and access time.
  • Payment Details: Subscription status (via Razorpay – no card is stored on our servers).

2. How We Use Your Information

MediCure uses your data to:

  • Provide personalized disease prediction and health recommendations.
  • Generate tailored diet and fitness plans.
  • Enable doctor consultations and appointment management.
  • Send notifications, updates, and reminders.
  • Improve and optimize the platform experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored securely using encrypted protocols. Sensitive health data is protected with strong access controls and backend authentication. Our team follows best practices in compliance with healthcare data security standards.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • Doctors can only view data related to patients who request consultations.
  • We may disclose anonymized usage data for analytics and platform improvement.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as your account is active or as needed for legal compliance. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and review your stored health data.
  • Correct or update any inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

MediCure is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie preferences via your browser settings.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Major changes will be notified via your registered email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@medicure.health
